Company & Product

With cutting-edge technology, contemporary design, and top-notch security, Zé Express is transforming the way we purchase beverages. Zé Express, a smart fridge conveniently located within condominiums, offers the quickest and most cost-effective solution for obtaining your preferred drinks. It's the ideal blend of affordability and convenience.

To shop on our smart coolers, simply sign up on our app, register your credit card, tap the screen to unlock the cooler, select your preferred beverages, and close the door. Our AI will then analyze your purchase and complete the checkout process automatically with your registered card.

The Challenge

To tackle two of our business's key challenges—customized promotions and streamlined refund processes—through a unified feature, optimizing both development time and human resources.

Project Goals

Boost purchase frequency

Encourage users who currently make an average of 3 purchases per month to buy more frequently.

Tailored promotions

Create customized promotions through various segmentation factors such as store location, purchase behavior, and customer preferences.

Efficient Refund

Streamline the refund process, which when managed by the current payment partner, takes up to 60 days to complete.

Research

What approaches do other companies take with digital wallets and transactions?

We conducted a benchmark analysis, investigating coupon usage in apps like iFood, Zé Delivery, and Rappi (our competitors in the Delivery category). We also closely examined transaction scenarios within popular digital wallets such as Starbucks and Uber.

Key findings encompass:
• The variety of payment methods available for purchasing digital funds
• The consistent placement of coupon selection at the checkout stage in delivery apps
• Automated selection of coupons or wallets by some companies
• The important role of Starbucks cashiers in reminding customers about using their digital wallet in a phygital context

What is our customer purchase behavior?

Through an in-app survey, we collected user feedback on promotions and payments. Out of 587 responses, we discovered that:


• 47% of respondents do not notice our promotions
• 34% are not interested in the discounted items
• 71% desire more payment options
• Promotions or discounts rank as the second most influential purchase factor

Build

To minimize friction in our purchase journey, our old purchase process did not have any payment selection. All payment actions were handled in a separate section of our app. However, unlike our benchmarked competitors who have a digital 'checkout' process, we needed to integrate payment actions on our home screen. This would allow users to opt for their digital wallet as a payment method before proceeding with their purchase.

To create a sense of familiarity akin to coupon usage, our promotion funds operate similarly. Users need to enter a personalized code to add promotional funds to their wallets.

Usability tests

To evaluate this new feature, we carried out a usability test involving 7 users, both new and existing.

The key takeaway from the test was that our existing users, accustomed to the previous purchase process, quickly bypassed our home screen, overlooking the new payment selection feature. In contrast, new users easily discovered this feature.

Based on this insight, we recognized the necessity for an onboarding campaign to introduce our users to this new feature.

Development

In addition to introducing new screen designs, adjustments were required in our backend purchase flow checks. We also had to integrate our refund and rewards back-office platform with the user app to ensure a seamless credit exchange process. Consequently, we not only detailed this information in our design handoff file but also held a dedicated meeting to verify all backend connections were accurately accounted for.

Improvement points

It's not clear to our existing users that they can now select and set their payment method before the purchase process. As a result, although we observed 38% fewer customer tickets for payment issues, we also noticed a new surge of 11% in tickets from users struggling to effectively utilize their wallet credits.

Increase in customer loyalty: Rewards Test

In a user survey conducted for a separate project, we experimented with utilizing digital credits as a reward for survey completion. Surprisingly, out of the three reward options available, an overwhelming 91% of customers opted for digital credits as their preferred reward.

Next steps

A seamless user experience

We are currently engaged in fresh research to enhance the purchase/payment process, aiming to provide a smoother method for users to choose their preferred payment option during purchase.

Enhanced financial accessibility

As we explore new payment options, we are actively developing the ability for users to purchase credits through Pix (Brazilian instant payment ecosystem). This initiative will enable individuals without access to debit or credit cards to shop conveniently at our smart fridges.
